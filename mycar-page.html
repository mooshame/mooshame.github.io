All men's dreams start here.

Which one do you prefer to drive?

An exciting array of choices encompassing various powertrains: electric, plug-in hybrid, and gasoline models. Everything from sedans and SUVs to MPVs is available. The line-up includes a multitude of thrilling technologies: mechanical supercharging, turbocharging, ISG motors, ERAD integrated rear axle motors, silicon carbide motors, DFV dual flow valve technology, and more...

Currently, Eden mainly drives Volvo S90 T8 Inscriptions, which integrates the vast majority of internal combustion engine era technologies into an exhilarating package. Eden periodically posts updates about each car, including but not limited to: vehicle modifications, popularizing vehicle knowledge, and studying vehicle technology. Like a man's own little garage, this place has all the tools you need, allowing you to work and learn in your self-built garage to your heart's content.
